# Task: Creative Writing

## What it demands
Voice, coherence over long outputs, and instruction following for style constraints. Raw benchmark intelligence correlates weakly with pleasing prose; human preference matters more. Long-form fiction needs large context to keep plot consistent. Marketing copy needs strong instruction following more than brilliance.

## Which benchmarks predict quality
- livebench_language (includes plot handling and paraphrase tasks) and livebench_if (story generation task) are the closest signals in our catalog.
- Arena Elo style rankings reflect taste but are not in our catalog data. Try 2-3 models on your actual prompt, style preferences are personal.

## Typical model tier
- Social copy, product descriptions: budget to mid tier.
- Novels, screenwriting, distinctive voice: frontier tier, or whichever model's voice you prefer.
- Local: very usable from 9B up for drafts; the writing is serviceable, not distinctive.

## Example user phrasings
- "help me write my novel", "punch up my marketing copy"
- "write Instagram captions", "generate story ideas"
- "a co-writer for my screenplay"
